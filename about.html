About Us

We are premier and professional chess training academy in Mumbai and Pune with a true sense of grass root level chess focus with initiatives being taken at Urban and Rural levels.
We have trained numerous students to compete in chess tournaments at their respective school, locality, town, city, state, national orinternational chess events.
We use the best in class software's, books, coaching methods, equipments and state of the art computers for analysis to meet your kids chess needs.

Vision

Chess is not just a game, but a healthy state-of-mind acquired through rigotous training and discipline.
Through an innovative approch uniquely our own, the Gambit Chess Academy offers a learning expeience that sets the standard for excellence in chess instruction. Integrating cutting-edge chess learning tools and technologies, our curriculum reflects children's interests and builds on their competencies.

Beginners

Teach how to play chess from start to finish, Basic Principles of the game and tactics.

Intermediate

Overview of opening, building of concious plan, higher level tactics, basic endgames, etc.

Advance

Formation of opening repertoire, middle game strategy, advance endgame techniques, etc.




Calculation

Chess helps students to focus and improves their calculating skills which helps them in their academics.
